Collecting Bee Venom

Дата: 26.11.2025 | Автор: Дворников М.Д.

Bees make a venom. It is important to people. They collect it to make medicines. They use it to study the human brain, too. An Australian scientist wants to protect bees. He finds a new way to collect bee venom. He gives the bees a piece of glass. The bees sting the glass. Glass is flat. The bees cannot get stuck anywhere. They cannot get hurt.

Bear attacks in Japan

Дата: 20.11.2025 | Автор: Дворников М.Д.

In northern Japan, many bears come near towns. They hurt people. Police must cull some bears. Japanese workers go to Montana in the US to learn good ideas. Many bear expert workers live there. They help people and animals every day. The workers use electric fences and clean areas with garbage. They also talk to the community and tell people how to stay safe. When people see a bear, they call the workers and get help fast.

A Czech man changes the world of 3D printing

Дата: 11.11.2025 | Автор: Дворников М.Д.

Josef Průša is a Czech inventor. He changes the world of 3D printing. As a student, he likes working with music equipment. He needs special parts for his projects. So, he decides to build his own 3D printer. His design becomes very popular. In 2012, he starts a company called Prusa Research in Prague. Today, the company sells printers all over the world and works with big organizations like NASA and Mercedes. Many small businesses also use his printers to make creative products.
